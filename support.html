Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click 'Forgot Password' on the login page and follow the verification steps via your registered email or phone.

What is the maximum transfer limit?

Daily transfer limit is ₹ 5 lakhs. Monthly limit is ₹ 20 lakhs. You can request to increase limits in Settings.

How long does a transfer take?

NEFT transfers take 1-2 hours. IMPS transfers are instant. International transfers take 2-3 business days.

Is my account secure?

Yes. We use 256-bit SSL encryption, two-factor authentication, and real-time fraud monitoring for all accounts.

How do I report fraudulent transactions?

Contact our support team immediately at 1800-BANK-001 or through the 'Report Issue' option in Settings.
